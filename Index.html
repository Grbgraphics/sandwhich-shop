About Agrusas Sandwich Shop

Agrusas Sandwich Shop has been serving the best sandwiches in Escondido for over 30 years. We are a family-owned and operated business that takes pride in using only the freshest ingredients to create our delicious sandwiches. Our menu features a wide variety of sandwiches, salads, and sides, all made to order and served with a smile.
